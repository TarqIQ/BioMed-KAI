Typical workflow

From question to verified answer

BioMed-KAI is designed to guide biomedical teams from initial query to validated insight with auditability along the way.

1. Pose your question

Use the AI chat or CLI to submit domain-specific prompts or tasks.

2. Agentic coordination

Evidence, reasoning, and safety agents collaborate, backed by graph-enriched retrieval.

3. Validate and iterate

Review analysis dashboards, inspect justification snippets, and iterate with reproducible CLI runs.
