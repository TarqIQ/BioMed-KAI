Highlights & key takeaways

Summaries of the most impactful results from the benchmarking, error analysis, and optimization studies.

Performance

CARE-RAG delivers 39.3% uplift

Agent accuracy improves significantly with the CARE-RAG pipeline, particularly for complex, multi-hop questions that demand graph-backed reasoning. Bootstrap sampling confirms consistent gains across 10,000 iterations.

Error mitigation

Failure patterns are recoverable

Error clustering reveals three dominant failure categories. Intervention with safety and reflection agents recovers 67% of cases, reducing severe failure modes to single digits.
