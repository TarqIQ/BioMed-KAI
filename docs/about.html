About the BioMed-KAI Platform

BioMed-KAI is a purpose-built agentic AI platform that integrates retrieval-augmented reasoning with a dynamic biomedical knowledge graph. It is engineered for real-world clinical workflows, blending autonomous agents with verifiable evidence and peer-reviewed validation.

Multidisciplinary team Peer-reviewed 200M+ graph edges

Mission

Transform precision medicine

Deliver evidence-grounded, real-time decision support while maintaining transparency and auditability. BioMed-KAI is crafted as a production-grade platform rather than a one-off research prototype.

Focus areas

Clinical reasoning, knowledge synthesis, translational research.

Designed for hospitals, research labs, and industry partners.

Why BioMed-KAI matters

The platform combines autonomy, biomedical grounding, and reproducibility to bridge the gap between research and clinical adoption.

Autonomous, agentic AI

Coordinated agents perform evidence gathering, reasoning, and safety oversight with reflective cycles to improve answer quality.

Biomedical knowledge graph

A Neo4j-backed graph integrates 21 databases and 15 ontologies to ensure responses are grounded in curated biomedical data.

Peer-reviewed & reproducible

The platform is documented in a peer-reviewed publication, and every experiment can be reproduced via the CLI and analysis portal.

Platform evolution

Milestones that shaped BioMed-KAI from foundational research to a validated platform.

Phase 1: Foundation

Comprehensive literature review and challenge identification for biomedical knowledge integration.

Phase 2: Knowledge graph

Neo4j-based graph built with 200M+ relationships spanning diseases, drugs, genes, and outcomes.

Phase 3: Agentic intelligence

Agentic orchestration implemented with reflective learning and retrieval-augmented reasoning.

Phase 4: Platform deployment

Production-ready platform shipped with FastAPI, WebSocket streaming, and Dockerized services.
